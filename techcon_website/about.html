History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small meetup for software enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated global technology conferences, attracting thousands of participants from all over the world.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and promote learning in the tech community. TechCon brings together developers, designers, and thinkers to share knowledge and build the future together.
